FAQ

What are unit counters in Age of Empires 2?

Unit counters in Age of Empires II are units that deal bonus damage to — or are more cost-efficient against — specific enemy unit types. Understanding counters is one of the most important skills in Age of Empires 2, letting you win engagements even when outnumbered.

What's the difference between a hard counter and a soft counter?

A hard counter deals specific attack bonus damage against a unit class — for example, Spearmen vs. Cavalry. A soft counter wins the matchup through general cost-efficiency or favorable stats without a dedicated bonus, such as Hussars against Monks.

How do I counter Knights in Age of Empires 2?

The best counters to Knights are Pikemen and Halberdiers (bonus damage vs. cavalry), Camel Riders (bonus damage vs. cavalry), and Monks (conversion). Crossbowmen also work well in numbers. See the full cavalry counter guide.
