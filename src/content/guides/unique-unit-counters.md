Unique Units are civilization-specific and often **break the standard counter rules**. A Cataphract
ignores Halberdier bonus damage. A Huskarl laughs at Arbalests. A Shrivamsha Rider dodges projectiles
entirely. This guide covers the unique units you'll face most in ranked play and exactly what counters
them.
